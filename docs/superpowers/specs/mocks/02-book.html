The Travelogue

Day one in the valley. The road from the harbor smelled of salt and pine, and the librarian — a sleepy cat named Mallory — pressed this empty book into my hands.

Things I saw

  • A waterwheel that hums in C
  • The night market beneath the bridge
“Write it down, or the valley forgets it for you.”

Tonight

I traded three plums for a tallow candle and sat by the arched window to write. The ink is the colour of wet bark.
